About WellRise

Let the goose guide your way to wellness!


In today’s fast-paced world, people often neglect their most basic need: sleep. The constant pressure to stay productive leads many to sacrifice rest, even though research from the Mayo Clinic shows that adults need at least 7 hours of sleep to function at their best.

But when sleep takes a back seat, it doesn’t just cause fatigue—long-term sleep deprivation can lead to serious health problems like weakened immunity, cognitive decline, and a higher risk of heart disease and diabetes.

One simple way to tackle this? Sleep tracking! Studies show that tracking your habits helps you make better decisions. By becoming more aware of sleep patterns, people can spot issues and make changes to improve their rest.

However, almost all the current sleep tracking technologies require the consumer to report their sleeping time and waking time to a screen. Which poses another problem, increased screen time.



Enter:
The WellRise Clock!



Say goodbye to screen-based sleep tracking! The WellRise Clock is a game-changer. Designed as a cute, friendly goose, it tracks your sleep with a simple tap—no screens, no apps, just a quick tap on its head.



Why WellRise?

The simplicity of a tap, the beauty of avoiding screens, and the joy of waking up to a happy little goose—what’s not to love? The WellRise Clock makes sleep tracking fun and easy, all while helping you maintain a healthy sleep cycle.

How Does it Work?

Before sleep: Tap the goose’s head to start tracking.
When you wake up: Tap again, and voilà! Your sleep data is logged—no fuss, no blue light.

A Smile to Start Your Day!

The WellRise Clock isn’t just functional—it’s fun too! The design adds a bit of joy to your morning routine, greeting you with a smile as soon as you wake up. It’s like a personal cheerleader for your sleep!
